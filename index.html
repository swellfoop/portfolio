About Me

Hi, I'm Tom.

I am a front-end developer with a passion for design, and a drive to be my best self.

Based and educated in England, I achieved an MPhys in Physics and Nanotechnology in 2015. My favourite experiences at university were always related to technology: FORTRAN projects, Electricity & Magnetism and Nanotechnology lectures, and being captain to my competitive gaming team outside of studying.

Since gaining my MPhys, I have worked as the Systems Developer at Berkeley Homes in South East London, with duties spanning VBA development, SharePoint administration, HTML & CSS scripting, business analysis, data analysis, and in-house graphic design.

I spend a lot of my free time on Pluralsight and Udemy keeping up to date and continuing to develop my skills. I also experiment with new ideas on CodePen and sometimes I even get featured!
